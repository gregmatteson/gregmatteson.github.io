Hey everyone, Greg Matteson here. I'm a pianist/arranger/director/singer from Atlanta, GA and I want to share my stuff with you! Professionally speaking, I work as an accompanist and a director. I graduated from Emory University with a BA in Piano Performance in '13 and have since been doing all sorts of freelance work - including recording accompaniments, playing for weddings and parties, accompanying soloists and various ensembles, teaching high school chorus and private lessons, performing on tours, recording a cappella groups, founding and directing an a cappella group, writing arrangements, and making transcriptions. I'll be periodically posting some of my work here, and if there's enough demand I will produce more!
